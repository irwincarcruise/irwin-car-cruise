About the Cruise

The Irwin Car Cruise brings classic cars and community together in Downtown Irwin. Expect great vehicles, a fun atmosphere, and a simple check-in process on event day.

What you need to know

  • Spring 2026 — exact date/time coming soon
  • Pre-reg: $10 (recommended)
  • Day-of: $15, metered based on available spots
  • Participants should follow volunteer instructions for staging/parking

How people use this site

  • Get the basics (date, location, fee)
  • Tap Pre-Register and complete the form on a phone
  • Save the confirmation email for event day
  • Event staff use the Staff Check-In link day-of

FAQ

Quick answers. We’ll update these as Spring 2026 details are confirmed.

When is the Irwin Car Cruise?
Spring 2026 — exact date and hours are TBD.
How much does it cost?
Pre-registration is $10. Day-of entry is $15 and metered by available spots.
Do I have to pre-register?
No, but pre-registration is recommended. It helps us plan and it’s usually faster on event day.
What happens if we hit capacity?
Day-of entry pauses once we’re full. Pre-registration may have a cap too (we’ll post it here if needed).
Where do I check in?
Downtown Irwin / Main Street area. Exact check-in point and staging instructions will be posted closer to the event.
